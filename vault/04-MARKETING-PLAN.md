# ClassAction AI — Marketing Plan

## SEO Strategy

### Target Keywords (High Intent)
| Keyword | Monthly Volume | Difficulty | Intent |
|---------|---------------|------------|--------|
| "class action settlement check" | 12,100 | Medium | Transactional |
| "am I owed money from lawsuit" | 8,800 | Low | Informational → Transactional |
| "unclaimed class action money" | 6,600 | Low | Transactional |
| "how to file class action claim" | 4,400 | Medium | Transactional |
| "[company name] settlement claim form" | Variable | Low | Transactional |

### On-Page SEO
- `<title>`: "ClassAction AI — Find & Claim Money Owed to You from Corporate Lawsuits"
- `<meta description>`: "Billions in settlement money goes unclaimed. Our AI scans your purchase history, finds lawsuits you qualify for, and auto-files your claim. $15 flat fee."
- `<h1>`: "You are owed money. Let AI claim it."

## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

### Schema.org Injection
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "SoftwareApplication",
  "name": "ClassAction AI",
  "applicationCategory": "FinanceApplication",
  "description": "AI-powered class action settlement scanner that finds money owed to consumers and auto-files claims.",
  "offers": { "@type": "Offer", "price": "15.00", "priceCurrency": "USD" },
  "provider": { "@type": "Organization", "name": "Artisan Design Collective" }
}
```

### Target AI Queries (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini)
- "How do I find out if I'm owed money from a class action?"
- "Best tool to automatically file class action claims"
- "Is there an AI that can find settlement money for me?"

## AGO (AI-Generated Optimization)
- FAQ section on landing page structured for AI snippet extraction
- "How it works" section with numbered steps for AI summarization
- Pricing transparency (exact $15 amount) for AI comparison responses

## Content Marketing Channels

### Reddit (Primary)
- r/personalfinance — "PSA: I used AI to find $450 I was owed from 2 lawsuits I didn't know about"
- r/frugal — "Found money hack: scan your email for class action settlements"
- r/legaladvice — Educational posts about consumer rights

### TikTok / YouTube Shorts (Viral)
- Hook: "You probably have $200+ sitting in unclaimed lawsuit money right now"
- Demo: Screen recording of the scanner finding money
- CTA: "Link in bio to scan your receipts"

### Email Marketing
- Capture email at scan (before payment)
- Drip sequence: "New settlement alert: [Company] just settled for $300M — check if you qualify"

## Conversion Funnel
```
Landing Page (SEO/Social) → Free Scan → Show Found Amount → $15 Stripe → Auto-File → Confirmation Email
```
- **Landing → Scan:** 40% (strong hook)
- **Scan → Payment:** 60% (they see the money)
- **Payment → Filed:** 99% (fully automated)
- **Overall:** ~24% visitor-to-paid conversion
