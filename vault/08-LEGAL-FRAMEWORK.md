## Laws This App Leverages

### Federal
| Law | Citation | Usage |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Class Action Fairness Act | 28 U.S.C. § 1332(d) | Federal jurisdiction for large class actions |
| Rule 23 Fed. R. Civ. P. | Fed. R. Civ. P. 23 | Class certification and notice requirements |
| FDCPA | 15 U.S.C. § 1692 | Debt collection class actions |

### State
- **CA:** Consumer Legal Remedies Act (CLRA)
- **NY:** General Business Law § 349
- **IL:** BIPA — massive data privacy settlements ($650M Facebook)
