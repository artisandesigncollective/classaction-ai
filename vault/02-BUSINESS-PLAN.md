# ClassAction AI — Business Plan

## Market Analysis

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **$2.5B+** in unclaimed class action settlements annually in the US
- **158M** US adults are potential class members in at least one active settlement
- **Global:** EU, UK, Canada, and Australia have similar collective action frameworks

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- **50M** US adults who actively use email (required for receipt scanning)
- **Assume 1% awareness/conversion** = 500,000 potential users in Year 1

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- **Year 1 Target:** 24,000 users (2,000/month)
- **Revenue Target:** $360,000 ARR

## Revenue Model

### Primary: Flat Fee ($15/claim)
- User pays $15 after the scanner shows them the dollar amount they're owed
- Payment is collected BEFORE any AI processing or form submission
- Margin: ~95% (LLM inference cost per claim ~$0.30)

### Secondary: Premium Tier ($49/year subscription — Future)
- Continuous monitoring of new settlements
- Automatic filing when matched
- Priority deadline alerts

### Unit Economics
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue per user | $15.00 |
| Stripe fees (2.9% + $0.30) | $0.74 |
| LLM inference cost | $0.30 |
| Gross margin per user | $13.96 (93%) |

## Competitor Analysis

| Competitor | Model | Fee | Weakness |
|-----------|-------|-----|----------|
| TopClassActions.com | Manual directory | Free (ad-supported) | No automation, user files manually |
| ClassAction.org | News/info site | Free | No filing service at all |
| Claims recovery lawyers | Legal service | 30-50% of recovery | Absurdly expensive for small claims |
| DoNotPay | AI legal assistant | $36/year subscription | Broad focus, not specialized in settlements |

### Our Moat
1. **Price:** $15 flat fee vs. 30-50% contingency
2. **Automation:** Receipt scanning + auto-fill eliminates all user friction
3. **Specialization:** We ONLY do class actions — not a general legal tool
4. **Data advantage:** Every scan builds our settlement matching database

## Go-to-Market Strategy
1. **SEO/GEO:** Rank for "am I owed money from [company] lawsuit"
2. **Reddit/Social:** Post in r/personalfinance, r/frugal about unclaimed settlements
3. **TikTok/Shorts:** "Did you buy a [product]? You might be owed $500" viral hooks
4. **Referral program:** User gets $5 credit for every friend who files a claim
